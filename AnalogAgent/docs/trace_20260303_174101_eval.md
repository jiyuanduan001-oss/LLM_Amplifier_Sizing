### 3.5 Root Cause Diagnosis

**M3 (TAIL) is in the linear region:**

- VDS_M3 = **70 mV**, Vov_M3 = **161 mV** → needs VDS ≥ Vov for saturation
- I_tail = 128.3 µA (only ~60% of designed 201 µA) — mirror ratio 20:1 with Ib=10 µA starves the bias node

**Fix:** Increase **Ib from 10 µA → 50 µA** to reduce the mirror ratio from 20:1 to ~4:1, giving M3 more VDS headroom.

## 6. Key Design Decisions

1. **L = 5.0 µm for all roles** — The LUT A0_upper sweep showed this is the only L where the intrinsic gain ceiling (42.0 dB) clears the 40 dB spec. Shorter L is impossible for this gain target on SKY130 at gm/ID = 12 S/A.

2. **Ib increased 10 µA → 50 µA (Iteration 2)** — The initial 20:1 mirror ratio forced M3 into the linear region (VDS = 70 mV, Vov = 161 mV). Raising Ib reduces the ratio to 4:1 and restores 32 mV of VDS headroom with M3 in saturation.

3. **No Miller compensation needed** — fp2 = 79.5 MHz is 7.7× GBW. PM = 65.3° is achieved without additional compensation capacitor.

4. **SPICE A0 (47.6 dB) > LUT A0_upper (42.0 dB)** — Expected: the LUT formula uses only gds from DC sweep, missing gmb, bulk-effect gain paths, and partial current-mirror degeneration. SPICE is the ground truth; LUT-based A0_upper is used only for L pre-selection.
